About Jewel Hub

Modernizing jewelry retail for Nepal

JewelHub is a jewelry shop management software product by Biplob Devkota, created to help Nepali jewelers move from manual registers and scattered spreadsheets to a reliable digital operating system.

The mission is simple: make high-value retail workflows easier to track, faster to operate, and clearer for owners, staff, karigars, and customers.
