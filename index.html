Jewel Hub: software for jewelry shop operations

Jewel Hub is the main product built by the BEE IT Solutions team. It is designed around the way jewelry shops work in Nepal, including stock weight, billing, customer balances, karigar jobs, purchases, and branch reporting.

The goal is to replace scattered notebooks and spreadsheets with one clear system that shop owners and staff can use every day.

Jewel Hub features

Everything a jewelry business needs to run cleanly

From daily billing to multi-store reporting, Jewel Hub keeps high-value operations visible, traceable, and easy to manage.

Inventory Management

Manage gold, diamond, silver, stones, categories, weights, and item movement with reliable stock visibility.

Billing & Invoicing

Create fast, accurate bills with GST/VAT-ready workflows, making charges, discounts, and payment records.

Sales Tracking

Track daily sales, customer history, shop performance, and counter activity from one dashboard.

Kaligadh Tracking

Monitor gold melting, refining, purity process, issued materials, and received balances with clarity.

Karigar Management

Track artisan work, wages, job-work, pending balances, delivery timelines, and workflow efficiency.

Purchase Management

Track purchases, suppliers, costs, purchase source, and inventory impact from one organized workflow.

Reports & Analytics

Get real-time insights, business reports, sales summaries, purchases, dues, and performance trends.
